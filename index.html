Pros and Cons of Dating Me

Pros

1. Coz I love you a lot.

2. I am a one-woman man.

Cons

I am terrible at remembering names. I always forget where I put my headphones. I accidentally set alarms for PM instead of AM. I leave my laptop charger behind everywhere I go. I get too excited about small things. I don’t fold my clothes properly. I leave books half-read. I overwater my plants. I always ask for “five more minutes” when waking up. I leave the toothpaste cap off. I get too invested in fictional characters. I’m bad at keeping track of my to-do list. I lose my wallet too often. I crack my knuckles too much. I can’t resist buying gadgets I don’t need. I hoard stationery. I accidentally put milk before cereal sometimes. I zone out during conversations. I eat way too many snacks in one sitting. I’m stubborn about picking the wrong route while driving. I always forget to clean my microwave. I re-watch the same shows over and over again. I take forever to decide on what to order at restaurants. I leave my phone on silent and forget about it. I get distracted while cleaning and start something else. I never fully close drawers or cupboards. I lose my socks after every wash. I have trouble figuring out left from right in a rush. I sometimes skip meals and regret it later. I’m terrible at keeping track of birthdays. I eat pizza with too much hot sauce. I spend too much time deciding which movie to watch. I always carry too many things in my pockets. I prefer late nights and hate waking up early. I leave my shoes near the door instead of putting them away. I laugh way too hard at bad jokes. I take too many detours on road trips. I write long messages and delete them before sending. I have way too many tabs open in my browser at all times. I always forget to refill the water bottle. I’m bad at multitasking and end up doing nothing. I love spicy food but complain when it’s too spicy. I tell stories that are longer than they need to be. I organize my workspace, only for it to get messy again the next day. I talk to myself when I think no one’s listening. I always forget the second verse of a song. I laugh at my own jokes, even when they’re not funny. I take way too long to finish books. I stay up late researching random facts I don’t need to know. I misplace my phone and then panic for hours. I get too attached to inanimate objects like my favorite mug.
